The Company

Drunk and Hungry is an online delivery company based out of Athens, GA. We deliver a variety of party supplies and convenience store goods. We have multiple drivers roving around your delivery area. Each driver has all of the inventory we offer in their car with them at all times. When you order online we dispatch the nearest driver to you. This way we are able to deliver fun stuf in record time.
